Photos & Bio

The Nest is a five-piece folk rock band based in San Francisco with a chilled out acoustic sound reminiscent of Crosby, Stills, & Nash, Okkervil River, and Delta Spirit. With song styles ranging from quiet guitar ballads to foot-stomping rock songs to something that might be reggae, their signature three-part harmony adds an unmistakably old-school approach to modern folk music.

Eric Shaw and Genny Krause provide lead vocals, banjo, mandolin, and rhythm guitar. Fred Hatfull plays tasty and tasteful electric guitar leads, while Etan Berkowitz holds down the low end on bass. Steve Johnson keeps time on the drums and often adds his voice to the sonic landscape. Weather permitting, someone may or may not also play a cello.
